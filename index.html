FAQs

Do you work under a JDA? What share is typical?
Yes. Share depends on location, approvals, and who funds costs; area or revenue share models are used.
Do you manage TDS on rent for tenants paying > ₹50,000/month?
We guide tenants on 194‑IB (one‑time yearly TDS with 26QC/16C) and issue owner statements.
Are you RERA‑aware and e‑stamp ready?
Yes—RERA thresholds, escrow discipline, Kaveri 2.0 e‑stamp and EC workflows are standard.
How do you pay owners/vendors?
RazorpayX bulk payouts with UTR and maker‑checker controls.
